Vision & Strategy

Financial OS ambition and strategic direction

Vision

Enterprises should grow without proportionally growing finance and operations teams. Intelligent automation should reduce financial and operations FTE requirements while improving efficiency, accuracy, governance, and scalability.

End State

Empower every enterprise to operate its financial organization through a single Financial Operating System, allowing teams to focus on creating business value rather than managing financial operations.

Strategic Pillars

Build reusable platform capabilities, clarify ownership across functions and squads, improve operational quality, and connect customer needs to product delivery with clear accountability.

High-Level Strategic Metrics

Track activation speed, reliability, customer readiness, service adoption, operational efficiency, and the ability to scale without proportional manual effort.

Suggested Operational Metrics

Monitor issue resolution, customer blockers, service quality, product dependencies, ownership clarity, and repeated operational patterns that should become product or process improvements.

Strategic Focus

High-level objectives for the line and teams

Line Goals

  • Improve activation speed and customer readiness across priority markets.
  • Increase product reliability, clarity, and operational quality.
  • Create a clear bridge between market needs, product delivery, and daily operations.

GTM Squads Goals

  • Own market focus and customer-facing priorities.
  • Surface blockers, demand signals, and urgent feedback quickly.
  • Coordinate customer communication with accurate product and operational context.

Product Squads Goals

  • Own product outcomes, roadmap delivery, and technical readiness.
  • Translate market and operational feedback into product improvements.
  • Keep GTM and operations aligned on releases, dependencies, and limitations.

Transaction Operations Goals

  • Bridge GTM and Product Squads for daily unplanned operational topics.
  • Track transaction, activation, bank-service, and product behavior issues.
  • Ensure operational feedback reaches the right product and engineering owners.

Customers Operations Goals

  • Bridge Legal and Finance topics into GTM execution.
  • Support customer-facing contract, billing, payment, and documentation coordination.
  • Reduce ambiguity in customer communication around legal and financial requirements.

Shared Functions Goals

  • Provide specialized review and support through defined function heads.
  • Keep daily interactions focused, traceable, and routed through clear owners.
  • Support the line without duplicating governance or formal planning processes.
